# The-Calico-Z

# Summary

A space for documents in regards to my 1978 280z, and a place to track project issues in relation to getting the car roadworthy again.

# History

In the summer of 2015 I purchased a 280z 2+2 and drove it 5 hours home.  I've picked at it over the years trying to get it roadworthy again but life has always gotten in the way, but no longer is this the case.  A number of friends new and old have breathed new life into the project for me over the last year or so and we've been making efforts as a group to move the project along.  I'm acting as if this group of friends is holding me accountable to moving on the project and am giving them weekly updates on what I intend to work on to make sure I stay motivated and actually take action on completing the project.

# Current Snapshot

As it sits the car has the engine removed and I'm prepping to have the engine worked over by a local Z enthusiast.  The engine smokes badly and has a number of broken manifold fastners that will be addressed during this work.
